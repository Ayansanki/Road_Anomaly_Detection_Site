Welcome to Road Anomaly Detection

Road Anomaly Detection is an intelligent computer vision-based platform designed to automatically detect road damage from user-provided images and videos. Using advanced deep learning techniques and a fine-tuned YOLOv8 model, our system can quickly identify potholes, cracks, and other road anomalies with high accuracy.
Whether you're a road maintenance authority, a smart city developer, or simply a citizen wanting to contribute to safer roads, our platform makes reporting and analyzing road conditions seamless and efficient. Just upload your data, and let our AI take care of the rest!

Get Started

To begin using the Road Anomaly Detection platform, you can navigate to the following sections:

  • Upload Report: Go to the Upload Report page to submit images or videos of road conditions for analysis.
  • View Reports: Visit the View Reports page to see previously submitted reports and their analysis results.

If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to reach out through our contact page.
